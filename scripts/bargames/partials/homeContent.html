Find games fast.

Find the games that you want to watch wherever you are. Businesses can advertise that they are showing the next big game, or you can see where and what people are watching through their check-ins.

Meetup with friends.

It's now even easier to coordinate meetups with your circle of friends. Just share your game plan on Facebook using our site and all of your buddies will know where you are going to be on the big day.

Never miss a game.

Customize your account with your favorite sports and teams. We can send you notifications when a friend arrives at a bar and what bars are televising the games you're interested in.

Get rewarded.

With every check-in you have the opportunity to receive a badge or achievement. The more games you attend the more you are rewarded. Bars sometimes even offer promotions for our app users.
